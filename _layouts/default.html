City Business Center Limassol (CBCL)

A contemporary and professional working environment, offering a fast and flexible solution for your business.

WELCOME

Located across from the Limassol District Court House, offering spectacular 5th floor views of the sea and city, City Business Center Limassol (CBCL) sits prominently in the heart of Limassol’s business district. It offers fully serviced offices complemented by a full range of industry-leading support services and facilities and a professional office support team.

The centre boasts eight luxury, purpose-built offices with a central reception area, a kitchen, on-site parking and a host of other facilities and support services.

The offices feature a modern design, a resourceful layout, a professional operational approach and a peace-of-mind mentality.

ABOUT US

Management Team

CBCL is owned and managed by CGT (Estates & Investment) Ltd, a company of local professionals with over 30 years of experience in corporate, fiduciary, international banking and management & administration services in Cyprus and abroad. The centre works closely with an alliance of local and international highly specialised firms, which allow it to offer a wide range of tax, business and corporate services to its diverse clientele. Moreover, the centre’s Russian and CIS clients are handled by an in-house, fully fledged Russian desk staffed by experienced native Russian speakers. CBCL clients can take advantage of Cyprus’ highly attractive tax and corporate offerings, while at the same time, maintain their offices at the stepping stone to Europe, the Middle East, Asia and Africa. For more information on the various corporate, fiduciary and administration services that may also be provided, please visit www.chelcomanagement.com
