MARKETING COMMUNICATION

Get your company’s name out from the comfort of your office.
Our in-house Business and Marketing Communications team can handle any and all of your promotional needs, including the formulation of marketing strategies, creation of full Corporate IDs, such as letterheads and business cards, design and development of websites, event organisation and more.

Corporate Identity

The Corporate ID Package* includes the design and prepress preparation for:

  • Custom Company Logo
  • Letterheads
  • Envelopes (DL, A5 & A4)
  • Business Cards
  • Invoices
  • Receipts
  • Compliment slips
  • Notepads

* Printing of stationery is not included and can be arranged with local preferred printing houses. Quantities to be determined with client.

Registration in Cyprus business directories, corporate profiles, corporate gifts, multimedia presentations, AGMs, organisation of seminars and events etc., are also available.

Company Website Design & Development

The Company Website Design & Development Package must be combined with the Corporate ID Package and includes:

  • CMS-Based Website
  • Basic Template Design and Customisation*
  • Up to 10 pages
  • Stock Photography
  • Copy Writing (to be determined and authored in consultation with client)
  • Uploading

* On-line forms and log-ins, on-line payments and other custom programming are not included and can be arranged on request.

Web Hosting & Support

  • Domain Registration and web hosting, set-up of up to 10 e-mails & support
  • Basic website updating and maintenance
