Serviced offices

CBCL provides a contemporary and professional working environment, offering a fast and flexible solution for your business. Ideal for starting up a new company or a branch and for establishing a physical presence in Cyprus.

A range of fully managed and fully furnished offices are available for immediate occupancy, featuring a secure IT and access infrastructure, a kitchen, adjacent parking and professional office support services.

Located in the centre of Limassol, CT Business Center is only minutes away from banks, restaurants, shops, public transport, the beach and the Limassol Marina.

Our turnkey approach ensures that all you have to do is move in and you are in business.
